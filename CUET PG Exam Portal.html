About CUET PG

Common University Entrance Test for Postgraduate (CUET PG) is a national-level entrance examination conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA) for admission to various postgraduate courses in top universities across India.

CUET PG covers 157 subjects across six streams: Science, MTech, Language, Humanities, Common, and Acharya. It's a computer-based test (CBT) conducted in both English and Hindi, making it accessible to a wider pool of candidates.
